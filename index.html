About Me

Hey there! I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering at NIT Bhopal, but somewhere along the way, I found myself drawn toward the world of data. What started as curiosity soon turned into a passion—especially after my internship at IIT Ropar, where I got to work hands-on with real data problems. That experience opened my eyes to how much impact data can have when it’s analyzed with purpose.

Since then, I’ve been diving deep into tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel, Tableau, and even machine learning—building projects around customer churn, A/B testing, regression models, and interactive dashboards. I love working on problems where data meets business decisions, and I’m especially interested in making insights not just technically sound, but actually useful and actionable.

Whether it’s creating a recommender system, deploying a Streamlit app, or digging through messy datasets to find patterns, I enjoy the process of turning raw data into something meaningful. I believe the best insights come when you blend analytical thinking with real business context—and that’s the space I love working in.
